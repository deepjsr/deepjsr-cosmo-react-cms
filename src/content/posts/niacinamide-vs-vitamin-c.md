One of the most persistent skincare myths is that **niacinamide** and
**vitamin C** cancel each other out. They don't. Modern, well-formulated
actives get along just fine — and together they're a brightness powerhouse.

## What each one does

- **Niacinamide (B3)** calms redness, balances oil and refines the look of
  pores. It's the steady, supportive friend.
- **Vitamin C** brightens tone, fades the look of dark spots and defends
  against free radicals from pollution and sun.

## How to layer them

With Lumière, you have two easy options:

1. **Same routine, different times.** Vitamin C in the morning (under
   sunscreen), niacinamide at night with Renew Night Cream.
2. **Layered, with care.** If you prefer both in the AM, apply the lighter
   texture first, let it absorb for a minute, then follow with the next. Our
   stabilised Bright-C is buffered to pair comfortably with Hydra-Glow Serum.

## The takeaway

Don't fear combining actives — fear *overdoing* them. Start slowly, watch
your skin, and let consistency do the work. [Reach out](/contact) if you'd
like a tailored layering plan.
