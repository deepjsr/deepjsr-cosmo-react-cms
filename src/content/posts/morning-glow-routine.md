A great morning routine doesn't need ten steps. It needs the right three. Here's the sequence our community reaches for most mornings.

## 1. Cleanse (gently)

Start with a soft, non-stripping cleanser to remove overnight buildup. You're not trying to scrub — you're resetting the canvas.

## 2. Hydra-Glow Serum

Press two drops of our **2% hyaluronic acid + vitamin B5** serum into damp skin. Doing this while skin is still slightly damp helps lock in water for that dewy finish.

## 3. Moisturise + SPF

Seal it all with a lightweight moisturiser, then — non-negotiable — a broad-spectrum SPF. Glow means nothing if you're not protecting it.

> Pro tip: keep your serum in the fridge during summer for an extra-de-puffing step.

That's it. Three minutes, three products, one glow.
