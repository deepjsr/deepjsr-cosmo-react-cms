While you sleep, your skin shifts into repair mode. An evening ritual should
support that work — not fight it with harsh, stripping steps.

## Cleanse, softly

Remove the day with a gentle cleanser. If you wore sunscreen or makeup,
a first pass with an oil or balm makes the second cleanse effortless.

## Treat with intention

Two to three nights a week, press in **Retinol Renew Oil** — encapsulated
retinol that smooths texture without the sting. On other nights, let
**Hydra-Glow Serum** do the hydrating.

## Seal with Renew Night Cream

**Renew Night Cream** is the star of barrier repair: peptides signal
collagen, ceramides rebuild the moisture barrier, and you wake up with
softer, more resilient skin.

## Once a week: Barrier Repair Mask

Add the **Barrier Repair Mask** for a cushioning boost when skin feels
tired or reactive.

> Consistency at night matters more than intensity. A calm routine, repeated,
> outperforms the occasional heroic one.
