Niacinamide (vitamin B3) is one of the few ingredients almost everyone's skin tolerates well. Here's the short version.

## What it does

- **Calms redness** and supports the skin barrier
- **Refines the look of pores** over consistent use
- **Balances oil** without drying

## How to use it

Apply it after cleansing and before heavier creams. A concentration of **2–5%** is plenty — more isn't better here. Our Hydra-Glow Serum pairs it with hyaluronic acid so you get calm *and* hydration in one step.

## Can I layer it?

Yes. Niacinamide plays well with most actives. If you also use vitamin C, apply them at different times of day (C in the morning, niacinamide at night) to keep things comfortable.

Consistency beats intensity. Give it four weeks.
